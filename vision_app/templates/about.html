About Biosense AI

Biosense AI is an advanced AI-powered waste sorting system designed to improve waste management efficiency. The system accurately identifies and categorizes various types of waste, making recycling easier and reducing negative environmental impacts.

This project was created by two students from Thoại Ngọc Hầu High School: Nguyễn Võ Quang Quý and Nguyễn Thiện Nhân.
